Traffic data from UK 2011 Census.

Original data source: http://wicid.ukdataservice.ac.uk/cider/index.php (needs SSO)
- data in uk_commute_flow/
- Area codes for local authority districts (LAD): Local_Authority_Districts_(December_2019)_Names_and_Codes_in_the_United_Kingdom.csv

Processed data:
- uk_traffic_mergedflows.rds - UK wide, local authority distrinct (LTLA in England). 
- uk_traffic_lockdown.csv - traffic flows weekly during lockdown, as percentage relative to 1 Feb 2020.
- Contact: Read, Jonathan <jonathan.read@lancaster.ac.uk>

- https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.05.15.20102715v1
